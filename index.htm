Welcome to Flatland!

        To get a better understanding of dimensions imagine a fixed point.

It has no dimensions and is called adimensional.

        Then let's imagine another point and draw a line between them.

As you may know, a line has an infinite amount of points,all together have a lenght.

We use the same analogy for lines and we get a 2D plane.

It has a lenght and a width.

        Now let's put an infinite amount of planes one another,so we get 3D space.

It has lenght,width and height.

So with this analogy we can imagine that the fourth dimensional space is an infinite amount of 3D planes one over another.

        We can use the same method to add many more dimensions.

        Let's make a thought experiment.Imagine if we have a cross-section of a 2D plane.

And a side-view of a 3D shape that is going trough the plane.

For a suposed observer of the 2D plane,the object would seem to move.

It is a common misconception that the fourth dimention is time, when it isn't.

Time is abstract concept made byhumans to make sense of what is around them.We can say that the time is just movement in the 4D space,

as it is,in our example,the movement of 3D objects through a plane.

        It's like the world around us,even us,is in higher dimensions,and what

we experience in our lives is just the projection of them in third dimensional space.

We might be complex higher dimentional beings,we might be eternal,and the past self and future self might be present as one,

we just travel forward on so called axis of time.This is just hypothetically speaking :)
